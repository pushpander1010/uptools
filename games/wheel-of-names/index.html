Frequently Asked Questions

Is the spinner truly random?

Yes! The spinner uses JavaScript's built-in Math.random(), which provides high-quality pseudorandom numbers. The decelerating physics simulation transforms this randomness into an engaging visual spinning experience.

Can I paste hundreds of names?

Yes, the wheel dynamically renders and scales text depending on the number of inputs. While it can support over 500 items, we recommend keeping it under 100 for optimal text readability on the screen.

Does this tool work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! The wheel and editor layout adapt responsively to fit smartphone and tablet screens, ensuring a seamless touch-to-spin experience.
