Frequently Asked Questions

How does the friendship test work?

Player 1 answers all questions first, then hands the device to Player 2 who answers the same questions. Your compatibility score is based on how many answers match.

Is the test free?

Yes, completely free with no sign-up required. Scores are saved locally on your device only.

Can I share the test with my friend?

Yes! Use the Challenge Link button to copy the URL and share it with your friend so they can play on their own device.
