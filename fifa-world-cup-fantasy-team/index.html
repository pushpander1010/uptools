❓ FAQ

What is the FIFA World Cup 2026 Fantasy Team Builder?
The FIFA World Cup 2026 Fantasy Team Builder lets you build your dream starting XI from 60+ real players representing top nations like Argentina, France, Brazil, England, Spain, Germany, and more. Choose a formation, pick players by position, and save or share your team via URL.
How do I build my fantasy team?
First select a formation (4-4-2, 4-3-3, 3-5-2, or 4-2-3-1). Then click a player from the player pool organized by position (GK, DEF, MID, FWD), then click an empty position slot on the pitch to place them. Fill all 11 slots to complete your dream XI.
Can I save and share my team?
Yes! Your team is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. You can also generate a shareable URL with your team encoded in the link — send it to friends and compare your dream XIs.
What formations are available?
The tool supports four classic formations: 4-4-2 (4 defenders, 4 midfielders, 2 forwards), 4-3-3 (4 defenders, 3 midfielders, 3 forwards), 3-5-2 (3 defenders, 5 midfielders, 2 forwards), and 4-2-3-1 (4 defenders, 2 defensive mids, 3 attacking mids, 1 forward).
Which players are included?
The player pool features approximately 60 real players from top FIFA World Cup 2026 nations including Argentina, France, Brazil, England, Spain, Germany, Portugal, Netherlands, Italy, Belgium, Japan, Morocco, South Korea, and more — organized by position (Goalkeepers, Defenders, Midfielders, Forwards).
How is chemistry calculated?
Chemistry is calculated based on team completeness (all 11 slots filled) and nation diversity. Having players from multiple nations gives a bonus, while stacking too many from one nation reduces chemistry. A full team with good nation diversity can reach 100%.
