How to Download Snapchat Videos

  1. Open Snapchat and find the snap or story you want to download
  2. Long-press on the snap to get the share menu
  3. Copy the link or share it to get the URL
  4. Paste the URL into the input field above
  5. Select your preferred quality
  6. Click "Download" to save the video
