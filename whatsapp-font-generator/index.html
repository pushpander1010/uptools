❓ FAQ

How do I make text bold in WhatsApp?

Wrap your text with asterisks: *bold text*. Or use this tool to generate it automatically.

Can I combine multiple text styles in WhatsApp?

Yes! You can combine bold, italic, and strikethrough. For example: *_~text~_* creates bold, italic, and strikethrough.

Does WhatsApp font formatting work on all devices?

Yes, WhatsApp text formatting works on Android, iOS, and WhatsApp Web across all devices.
