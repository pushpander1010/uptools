Frequently asked questions

Can I download a WhatsApp DP using only a phone number?

No. WhatsApp doesn't expose profile pictures via phone number through any public API. You need the contact saved or their WhatsApp link. Our downloader works from a link or saved contact.

Is it legal to save someone's WhatsApp DP?

Saving a profile picture for personal viewing is generally fine, but reusing or sharing someone's photo without permission may violate their privacy rights. Respect others' settings.

Why is someone's DP not visible to me?

Their privacy is likely set to "My Contacts" or "Nobody", or they removed you. Private accounts hide DPs from non-contacts by design.
