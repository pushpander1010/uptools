✅ Do's and ❌ Don'ts for LinkedIn Profiles

✅ Do's

  • ✅ Do use a professional headshot for your profile photo.
  • ✅ Do write a headline with relevant keywords.
  • ✅ Do quantify achievements with numbers and metrics.
  • ✅ Do list 15+ relevant skills on your profile.
  • ✅ Do request recommendations from colleagues.
  • ✅ Do post valuable content regularly (1-2x per week).

❌ Don'ts

  • ❌ Don't leave your headline as just your job title.
  • ❌ Don't write long paragraphs in your about section.
  • ❌ Don't add irrelevant skills just to increase count.
  • ❌ Don't ignore connection requests from relevant contacts.
  • ❌ Don't post only self-promotional content.
  • ❌ Don't leave your profile incomplete or outdated.

This tool computes your LinkedIn profile strength entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to any server, ensuring your privacy.

The score is based on weighted factors: headline quality, about section length, experience count, skills, recommendations, and connections.
