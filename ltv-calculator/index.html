📚 Learn About LTV

🏦

What is LTV?

Loan-to-Value (LTV) is the ratio of your mortgage loan to the appraised value of the property. It tells lenders how much financial risk they're taking.


Formula:
LTV = (Loan Amount ÷ Property Value) × 100

🎯

Why 80% Matters

  • LTV below 80% = no PMI required
  • Better interest rates from lenders
  • More equity means financial security
  • Easier refinancing options
  • Lower monthly payment burden
📈

How to Improve LTV

  • Increase your down payment
  • Wait for property appreciation
  • Make extra principal payments
  • Request a new home appraisal
  • Home improvements that add value
