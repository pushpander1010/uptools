❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What timezone are FIFA World Cup 2026 matches scheduled in?

Most matches use the local time of the host venue across USA, Canada, and Mexico. Eastern Time (ET) is the most common reference since the majority of matches are in USA Eastern / Central venues.

How do I convert match times to Indian Standard Time (IST)?

Select "India (IST +5:30)" in the quick buttons above, or choose Asia/Kolkata from the dropdown. Your match table will automatically show all times in IST.

Does the converter handle Daylight Saving Time correctly?

Yes. The tool uses JavaScript's Intl.DateTimeFormat with the IANA timezone database, which accounts for DST transitions automatically. Your converted times will always reflect the correct offset for the match date.

When is the 2026 World Cup final and what time?

The final is on Sunday, July 19, 2026 at MetLife Stadium, New Jersey. Kickoff is expected around 4:00 PM ET. Use the converter above to see the exact time in your timezone.

Can I bookmark my converted schedule?

Currently the tool converts on-the-fly. You can print the table or take a screenshot once your timezone is selected. We recommend setting calendar alerts for must-watch matches.

How many matches are in the 2026 World Cup?

The 2026 tournament features 48 teams and 104 matches — the largest World Cup ever. Group stage runs June 11-June 27, Round of 32 starts June 28, and the final is July 19.
