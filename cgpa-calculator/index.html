Frequently Asked Questions

How is CGPA calculated?

CGPA = sum(grade points × credits) ÷ sum(credits) across all subjects.

Is this CGPA calculator free?

Yes. It runs fully in your browser with no sign-up required.

Does it support the 4.0 scale?

Yes. Enter grade points on your scale (0–10 or 0–4); the weighted formula works for any scale.
