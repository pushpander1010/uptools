FAQ

How do I convert hourly pay to annual salary?

Multiply hourly rate by hours per day, days per week and weeks per year. This calculator does that automatically using your schedule.

Can I use this for part-time or freelance work?

Yes. Change hours per day, days per week and weeks per year to match your real workload or contract.
