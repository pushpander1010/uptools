Frequently Asked Questions

Does it ignore case?

Yes — words are counted case-insensitively (Apple = apple).

Can I use it for keyword density?

Yes — it shows counts and percentages, ideal for SEO content checks.

Is it free?

Yes, fully free and runs in your browser.
