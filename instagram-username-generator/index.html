💡 Instagram Username Best Practices

✨ Memorable & Brandable

  • Keep it simple: Easy to spell and remember
  • Make it brandable: Reflects your personality or business
  • Avoid trends: Choose something timeless
  • Be consistent: Use same handle across platforms
  • Test pronunciation: Make sure it sounds good

🎯 Strategic Considerations

  • Include keywords: Help people find you
  • Consider SEO: Searchable terms work better
  • Think long-term: Will it work as you grow?
  • Check availability: Secure it on all platforms
  • Avoid copyright: Don't use trademarked names

📱 Technical Guidelines

  • Length limit: Maximum 30 characters
  • Allowed characters: Letters, numbers, periods, underscores
  • No spaces: Use dots or underscores instead
  • Case insensitive: @Username = @username
  • Unique requirement: Must be different from existing users
