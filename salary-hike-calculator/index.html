Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good salary hike?

A 10-15% annual hike is considered good. Top performers may get 20%+. Job switches typically command 20-40% hikes.

How do I negotiate a better hike?

Research market rates, document your achievements, quantify your impact, and have competing offers if possible.

Should I consider in-hand salary for hike?

Always negotiate on CTC as it's the standard. But also check the impact on take-home using the CTC Calculator.

✅ Do's and ❌ Don'ts

✅ Do's

  • ✅ Do research market rates before negotiating.
  • ✅ Do document your achievements and impact.
  • ✅ Do consider total compensation (bonus, stocks, benefits).
  • ✅ Do time your negotiation during performance reviews.

❌ Don'ts

  • ❌ Don't accept the first offer without negotiating.
  • ❌ Don't compare only CTC — check in-hand salary too.
  • ❌ Don't forget to account for PF and other deductions.
  • ❌ Don't burn bridges if the negotiation doesn't go well.
