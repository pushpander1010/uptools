Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

At UpTools, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • We do not require account signup or store personal data.
  • Basic analytics (page visits, device type) via privacy-friendly tools may be collected to improve services.
  • Some tools may store preferences locally on your browser (localStorage) - never sent to our servers.

2. Use of Cookies & Ads

  • UpTools itself does not set tracking cookies.
  • Third-party services (e.g., Google AdSense) may use cookies to serve relevant ads.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

3. Data Security

We implement best practices to keep our tools fast and secure. Since we don't collect sensitive data, risks are minimal.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to external sites (e.g., YouTube tutorials). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. Your Rights

You may request clarification or deletion of any data related to you (if ever collected). Contact us via the Contact page.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of UpTools means you accept the latest version.
