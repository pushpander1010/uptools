❓ FAQ

How do Snapchat streaks work?

A streak starts when you and a friend send snaps to each other for 3 consecutive days. The 🔥 emoji and number appear next to their name.

What happens if I lose a Snapchat streak?

If you don't send a snap within 24 hours, the streak ends. You can contact Snapchat support to restore streaks lost due to technical issues.

What's the longest Snapchat streak ever?

The longest verified Snapchat streak is over 2900+ days (8+ years). Maintaining long streaks requires daily commitment from both users.
