📥 Instagram Reels Downloader

Learn how to download Instagram Reels videos for free on Android, iOS, and PC. Save your favorite Reels to watch offline!

📱 Method 1: Using Third-Party Websites

Web Android iOS

The easiest way to download Reels without installing apps.

  1. Open Instagram and find the Reel you want to download
  2. Tap the three dots (•••) menu on the Reel
  3. Select "Copy link"
  4. Open a Reels downloader website (e.g., SaveInsta, SnapInsta, InstaDownloader)
  5. Paste the link into the download box
  6. Click "Download" and save the video
⚠️ Privacy Warning: Only use reputable downloader websites. Avoid sites that ask for your Instagram login credentials.

🤖 Method 2: Android Apps

Android

Use dedicated Android apps for faster downloads.

  1. Download a Reels downloader app from Google Play Store (e.g., "Reels Downloader", "Video Downloader for Instagram")
  2. Open Instagram and copy the Reel link
  3. Open the downloader app
  4. Paste the link and tap "Download"
  5. Video will be saved to your Gallery or Downloads folder

🍎 Method 3: iOS Shortcuts

iOS

Use the iOS Shortcuts app to download Reels on iPhone.

  1. Download a "Download Instagram Reels" shortcut from the Shortcuts Gallery
  2. Open Instagram and find the Reel
  3. Tap Share → More → Select the shortcut
  4. The Reel will download to your Photos app
💡 Pro Tip: Add the shortcut to your Share Sheet for quick access!

💻 Method 4: Desktop/PC

Web

Download Reels on your computer using browser extensions or websites.

  1. Open Instagram.com in your browser
  2. Find the Reel and copy its URL from the address bar
  3. Visit a Reels downloader website
  4. Paste the URL and click "Download"
  5. Choose video quality and save to your computer
