💬 WhatsApp Web Guide

Master WhatsApp Web with our complete step-by-step guide. Learn how to set up, use advanced features, and troubleshoot common issues.

Getting Started

Step 1: Open WhatsApp Web

Visit web.whatsapp.com in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

Step 2: Scan QR Code

Open WhatsApp on your phone → Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device. Scan the QR code on your screen.

Step 3: Start Messaging

Your chats will sync instantly. You can now send messages, make calls, and share media from your desktop.

Advanced Features

🔔 Enable Notifications

Allow browser notifications to get alerts for new messages even when the tab is in the background.

📱 Keep Phone Connected

Your phone must stay connected to the internet. WhatsApp Web works as a mirror of your phone.

🔐 Security Tips

Always log out on shared computers. Use "Log out from all devices" if you suspect unauthorized access.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use WhatsApp Web without my phone?

No, your phone must be connected to the internet. WhatsApp Web is a mirror of your phone, not a standalone app.

How many devices can I link?

You can link up to 4 devices simultaneously to your WhatsApp account.

Is WhatsApp Web secure?

Yes, all messages are end-to-end encrypted. Always log out on public computers for security.

Why is WhatsApp Web not working?

Check your internet connection, clear browser cache, or try a different browser. Restart your phone if needed.

💡 Tip: Use keyboard shortcuts like Ctrl+N to start a new chat and Ctrl+Shift+M to mute notifications.
