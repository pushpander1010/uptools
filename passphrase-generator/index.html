Frequently Asked Questions

What is a passphrase?

A sequence of random words that is long, strong and easier to remember than a password.

How secure are they?

Long random word combinations are highly resistant to brute-force and guessing attacks.

Is it generated locally?

Yes — entirely in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
