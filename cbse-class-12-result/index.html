About CBSE Class 12 Results

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) conducts the Class 12 board examinations twice a year. Results are typically declared within 4-6 weeks after the exams conclude.

What You'll See in Your Result:

  • Subject-wise Marks: Marks obtained in each subject out of the total
  • Grades: Letter grades (A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2, D, E) based on marks
  • Total Marks: Aggregate marks across all subjects
  • Percentage: Overall percentage scored
  • Status: Pass/Fail status
