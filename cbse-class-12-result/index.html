Before You Start

This page does not fetch live result data from CBSE servers. It helps you use the official result links and analyze your marks after you receive them.

Open Official CBSE ResultsOpen CBSE Website

Result Access Checklist

  • Keep your roll number, school number, admit card ID, and date of birth ready.
  • Cross-check every subject mark before sharing or applying anywhere.
  • Download the provisional scorecard and save a screenshot.
  • Use the original marksheet from school for final admissions when required.

What To Do Right After Results

  • Calculate your overall percentage and best-of-five percentage.
  • Shortlist courses and colleges based on stream and score band.
  • Check entrance exam eligibility instead of relying only on marks.
  • Keep migration, caste, domicile, and transfer certificates ready if needed.
