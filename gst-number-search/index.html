Digits 1–2
State Code (e.g. 27 = Maharashtra)
Digits 3–12
PAN Number of the taxpayer
Digit 13
Entity number (1–9, A–Z)
Digit 14
Always "Z" by default
Digit 15
Check digit (0–9 or A–Z)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a GSTIN?

GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number) is a 15-digit unique identifier assigned to every GST-registered business in India.

How do I verify a GST number?

Enter the 15-digit GSTIN in the search box. The tool will validate the format, decode the state code, PAN, entity type, and registration sequence.

What does a GSTIN contain?

A GSTIN has: 2-digit state code, 10-digit PAN, 1-digit entity number, 1-digit Z (default), and 1 check digit.
