❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is VAT?

VAT (Value Added Tax) is a consumption tax applied to goods and services in the European Union and many other countries. It's collected at each stage of production and distribution.

How do I calculate VAT?

To add VAT, multiply the net price by the VAT rate and add to the original. To remove VAT from a gross price, divide by (1 + VAT rate). For example, €100 with 20% VAT = €100 × 1.20 = €120.

Which European country has the highest VAT rate?

Hungary has the highest standard VAT rate in Europe at 27%, followed by Denmark, Sweden, Norway, and Croatia at 25%.
