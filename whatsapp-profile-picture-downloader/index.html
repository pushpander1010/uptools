Frequently asked questions

What resolution are WhatsApp profile pictures?

WhatsApp stores them at up to 640x640 pixels. Our tool fetches that full version, not the small thumbnail you see in chat.

Can I download a profile picture I can't see?

No. If their privacy is "My Contacts" or "Nobody", WhatsApp won't send the photo to your device. No tool can bypass that.

Is it legal to save someone's DP?

Saving a profile picture you can see for personal use is generally fine, but reusing or sharing it without permission may violate their privacy.
