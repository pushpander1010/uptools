## Design Pattern for Downloaders

All downloader tools should include:
1. **URL Input Field** - Paste video/content URL
2. **Quality Selector** - Multiple quality options
3. **Format Selector** - MP4, MP3, WebM, etc.
4. **Download Button** - Clear CTA
5. **Features List** - What makes it special
6. **How-to Guide** - Platform-specific instructions
7. **FAQ Section** - Common questions
8. **AdSense Slots** - Monetization
9. **Breadcrumbs** - Navigation
10. **Mobile Responsive** - Works on all devices
