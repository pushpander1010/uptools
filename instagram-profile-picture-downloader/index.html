Frequently asked questions

Can I download a private account's DP?

No — Instagram doesn't serve private profiles' photos to non-followers. Only public accounts work.

Do I need to log in?

No login for public profiles — just the username.

Is it legal?

Downloading a public profile picture for personal use is generally fine, but reusing someone's photo without permission may violate their rights.
