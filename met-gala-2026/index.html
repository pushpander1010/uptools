About Met Gala 2026

What is the Met Gala?

The Met Gala is an annual fundraising gala held for the benefit of the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Costume Institute in New York City. It's one of the most prestigious and exclusive fashion events in the world, known for its elaborate red carpet and celebrity attendees.

What was the 2026 theme?

The 2026 theme was "Fashion Is Art," celebrating the artistic expression and creativity in high fashion. Attendees wore looks that blurred the lines between wearable fashion and fine art.
