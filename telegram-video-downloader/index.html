How to Download Telegram Videos

  1. Open Telegram and find the message with the video
  2. Long-press on the message to open the context menu
  3. Select "Copy Link" or "Share" to get the message link
  4. Paste the link into the input field above
  5. Select your preferred quality (Original, 720p, 480p, or audio)
  6. Click "Download" to save the video
