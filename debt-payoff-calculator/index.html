Frequently Asked Questions

What is the debt snowball method?

The debt snowball method pays off debts from smallest to largest balance, regardless of interest rate. This provides quick wins and psychological motivation as you eliminate debts one by one.

What is the debt avalanche method?

The debt avalanche method pays off debts from highest to lowest interest rate. This saves the most money on interest but may take longer to see the first debt eliminated.

Which debt payoff method is better?

Avalanche saves more money on interest, but snowball provides faster psychological wins. Choose avalanche if you're motivated by savings, or snowball if you need quick victories to stay motivated.
