How to Download Instagram Videos

Download on Mobile (Android/iPhone)

  1. Open Instagram app and find the video/reel you want to download
  2. Tap the three dots (⋯) menu on the video
  3. Select "Copy link" or "Share"
  4. Come back to this page and paste the URL above
  5. Click "Download" button
  6. Video will be saved to your device's gallery

Download on Desktop

  1. Open Instagram.com in your browser
  2. Find the video/reel you want to download
  3. Click the three dots (⋯) menu
  4. Select "Copy link"
  5. Paste the URL in the input field above
  6. Click "Download" to save the video

Download from Instagram Web

  1. Visit Instagram.com and log in
  2. Navigate to the video you want to download
  3. Right-click on the video and select "Copy link address"
  4. Paste the link in the input field above
  5. Click "Download" button
  6. Video will download to your downloads folder
