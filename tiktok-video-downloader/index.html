📥 TikTok Video Downloader

Download TikTok videos without watermark for free. Save videos to watch offline on any device!

🌐 Method 1: Using Downloader Websites

Web Android iOS

The easiest way to download TikTok videos without watermark.

  1. Open TikTok app and find the video
  2. Tap the Share button (arrow icon)
  3. Select "Copy link"
  4. Open a TikTok downloader website (e.g., SnapTik, SSSTik, TikMate)
  5. Paste the link and click "Download"
  6. Choose "Without Watermark" option
  7. Save the video to your device
⚠️ Important: Never enter your TikTok password on third-party websites. Legitimate downloaders don't need your login.

📱 Method 2: TikTok's Built-in Save

Android iOS

Save videos directly from TikTok (with watermark).

  1. Open the TikTok video
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Select "Save video"
  4. Video will be saved to your Camera Roll or Gallery
💡 Note: This method includes the TikTok watermark. Use Method 1 for watermark-free downloads.

🍎 Method 3: iOS Shortcuts

iOS

Use the Shortcuts app to download TikTok videos on iPhone.

  1. Download a "TikTok Downloader" shortcut
  2. Open TikTok and find the video
  3. Tap Share → More → Select the shortcut
  4. Video downloads without watermark to Photos

💻 Method 4: Desktop/PC

Web

Download TikTok videos on your computer.

  1. Open TikTok.com in your browser
  2. Find the video and copy its URL
  3. Visit a TikTok downloader website
  4. Paste the URL and click "Download"
  5. Select quality and save to your computer

💡 Best Practices

  • Respect creators: Only download for personal use
  • Credit original: Always credit the creator if you share
  • Check permissions: Some creators disable downloads
  • Quality options: Choose HD quality when available
  • Storage: Videos can be large, check your storage space
  • Copyright: Don't use downloaded videos commercially without permission
