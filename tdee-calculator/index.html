📖 What is TDEE?

Your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the total number of calories your body burns each day, including all activity. It's calculated by multiplying your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate — calories burned at rest) by your activity level multiplier. Eating below your TDEE causes weight loss; eating above causes weight gain.
