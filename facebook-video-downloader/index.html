📥 Facebook Video Downloader

Download Facebook videos in HD quality for free. Save videos from Facebook to watch offline!

🌐 Method 1: Using Downloader Websites

Web Android iOS

The easiest way to download Facebook videos in any quality.

  1. Open Facebook and find the video
  2. Click the three dots (•••) menu on the video
  3. Select "Copy link"
  4. Open a Facebook video downloader website (e.g., FBDown, GetFBStuff, FBVideoDownloader)
  5. Paste the link into the download box
  6. Choose quality: SD or HD
  7. Click "Download" and save the video
⚠️ Security: Never enter your Facebook password on third-party websites. Legitimate downloaders don't need your login.

💻 Method 2: Desktop Browser

Web

Download Facebook videos directly from your browser.

  1. Open Facebook.com in your browser
  2. Find the video and right-click on it
  3. Select "Show video URL" or "Copy video URL"
  4. Paste the URL into a downloader website
  5. Download in your preferred quality

📱 Method 3: Mobile Apps

Android

Use dedicated apps for faster downloads on Android.

  1. Download a Facebook video downloader app from Play Store
  2. Open Facebook and copy the video link
  3. Open the downloader app
  4. Paste the link and tap "Download"
  5. Video saves to your Gallery

🍎 Method 4: iOS Shortcuts

iOS

Download Facebook videos on iPhone using Shortcuts.

  1. Download a "Facebook Video Downloader" shortcut
  2. Open Facebook and find the video
  3. Tap Share → More → Select the shortcut
  4. Video downloads to your Photos app

💡 Best Practices

  • Respect privacy: Only download public videos or videos shared with you
  • Credit creators: Always credit the original poster if you share
  • Quality matters: Choose HD for better viewing experience
  • Storage space: HD videos are larger, check your storage
  • Copyright: Don't use downloaded videos commercially without permission
  • Personal use: Download only for personal viewing

❓ FAQ

Can I download Facebook videos for free?

Yes, you can download Facebook videos for free using third-party downloader websites. No registration or payment required.

How do I download Facebook videos in HD?

Use a Facebook video downloader that offers HD quality options. Most downloaders let you choose between SD and HD quality before downloading.

You can only download videos that are publicly accessible or shared with you. Private videos from other users cannot be downloaded without permission.
