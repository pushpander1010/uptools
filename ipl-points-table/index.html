Frequently Asked Questions

How are IPL points calculated?

A win earns 2 points, a loss earns 0, and a no-result or tie earns 1 point each. Teams are ranked by total points, then by NRR if tied on points.

How many teams qualify for IPL playoffs?

The top 4 teams qualify. Teams ranked 1st and 2nd play Qualifier 1 (winner goes straight to the final). Teams ranked 3rd and 4th play the Eliminator.

What is NRR in IPL?

Net Run Rate = (Total runs scored / Total overs faced) − (Total runs conceded / Total overs bowled). A positive NRR means the team scores faster than it concedes.

Which team has won the most IPL titles?

Mumbai Indians (MI) hold the record with 5 IPL titles (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020). Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are second with 5 titles as well (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023).
