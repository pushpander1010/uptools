CBSE Result Guide 2026

How to check your 10th/12th marks and turn your CGPA into a percentage — fast and free.

Check your result

  1. Go to the official CBSE results portal.
  2. Enter roll number, school code, and admit card ID.
  3. Your subject marks and overall result appear instantly.

Convert CGPA to percentage

CBSE formula: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

Example: CGPA 9.2 × 9.5 = 87.4%. Use our calculator for any value:

Frequently asked questions

How do I check my CBSE result?

Use the official CBSE results portal with your roll number, school code, and admit card ID.

CGPA to percentage?

Multiply CGPA by 9.5. A 10 CGPA = 95%, 9 CGPA = 85.5%.

What's a good 12th percentage?

75%+ is solid for admissions; 90%+ unlocks top colleges and scholarships.
