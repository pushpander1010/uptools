How It Works

  1. Target Time — The countdown calculates the time remaining until July 20, 2026 at 00:00 UTC (July 19, 8:00 PM ET), which is the scheduled kick-off of the World Cup Final.
  2. Live Calculation — Your browser continuously computes the difference between the current time and the target time using JavaScript's Date.now().
  3. Real-Time Display — Every second, the countdown updates the days, hours, minutes, and seconds displayed on screen and in the browser tab title.
  4. Automatic Sync — The timer runs entirely client-side and respects your device's local time. No page refresh needed — it adjusts automatically.
  5. Zero Latency — When the Final begins, the timer hits zero and displays a celebration message so you know it's game time!

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the FIFA World Cup 2026 Final?
The FIFA World Cup 2026 Final was held on July 19, 2026, at 8:00 PM ET at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey, USA. Spain beat Argentina 1–0 to win their second title.
How does the countdown timer work?
The countdown timer calculates the time between your current local time and the target time (July 20, 2026 00:00 UTC). It updates in real-time every second using JavaScript, displaying days, hours, minutes, and seconds remaining.
How many teams are competing in World Cup 2026?
For the first time in history, the FIFA World Cup features 48 teams — expanded from 32 teams. The group stage consists of 12 groups of 4 teams each, with the top two and eight best third-placed teams advancing to the Round of 32.
Which countries are hosting the 2026 World Cup?
Three countries co-hosted the 2026 World Cup: the United States, Mexico, and Canada. It was the first World Cup hosted by three nations jointly. The Final was played in the USA at MetLife Stadium, where Spain beat Argentina 1–0.
How many matches are in the 2026 World Cup?
A total of 104 matches were played — the most in any FIFA World Cup edition. This was due to the expanded 48-team format with 72 group-stage matches and 32 knockout-stage matches. Spain won the Final 1–0 against Argentina.
Where is the Final being played?
The World Cup 2026 Final is at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey, USA. It has a seating capacity of over 82,000 and is just outside New York City.
