Frequently Asked Questions

What is net worth?

Net worth is the total value of your assets (what you own) minus your liabilities (what you owe). It's a snapshot of your financial health at a specific point in time.

What is a good net worth by age?

A general rule: your net worth should equal your age times your annual income divided by 10. For example, at age 40 with $75K income, target net worth is $300K. However, this varies widely based on individual circumstances.

Should I include my home in net worth?

Yes, include your home's current market value as an asset, and your remaining mortgage balance as a liability. The difference (your home equity) contributes to your net worth.
