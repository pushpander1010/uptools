Frequently Asked Questions

What is an IFSC code?

IFSC (Indian Financial System Code) is a unique 11-character code assigned to every bank branch in India for electronic fund transfers.

How many IFSC codes can I search at once?

You can search unlimited IFSC codes. Just paste them one per line.

Can I download the results?

Yes, click the "Download CSV" button to export results as a spreadsheet file.
