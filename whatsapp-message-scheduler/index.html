⏰ WhatsApp Message Scheduler

Learn how to schedule WhatsApp messages for free on Android, iOS, and WhatsApp Business. No third-party apps needed.

📱 Method 1: WhatsApp Business (Built-in)

WhatsApp Business

WhatsApp Business has built-in scheduling for quick replies and away messages.

  1. Open WhatsApp Business app
  2. Go to Settings → Business tools
  3. Tap Away message or Quick replies
  4. Enable scheduling and set your time
  5. Compose your message and save
⚠️ Note: This only works for automated responses, not individual scheduled messages.

🤖 Method 2: Android Built-in Scheduler

Android

Use Android's native message scheduling feature (available on Samsung, Google Pixel, and some other brands).

  1. Open WhatsApp and select a chat
  2. Type your message
  3. Long-press the Send button
  4. Select Schedule message
  5. Choose date and time
  6. Confirm to schedule
⚠️ Note: Feature availability depends on your Android device manufacturer.

🍎 Method 3: iOS Shortcuts

iOS

Use the iOS Shortcuts app to create automated WhatsApp messages.

  1. Open the Shortcuts app on iPhone
  2. Tap + to create a new shortcut
  3. Search for "Send Message" action
  4. Select WhatsApp as the app
  5. Enter recipient and message
  6. Tap Automation → Time of Day
  7. Set your schedule and save
⚠️ Note: iOS automations may require confirmation before sending.

💡 Best Practices

  • Test first: Send a test message to yourself before scheduling important messages
  • Time zones: Double-check time zones when scheduling international messages
  • Battery: Keep your device charged and connected to ensure messages send
  • Internet: Ensure stable internet connection at scheduled time
  • Backup: Keep a copy of important scheduled messages

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I schedule WhatsApp messages for free?

Yes! WhatsApp Business has built-in message scheduling. For personal WhatsApp, you can use Android's built-in scheduler or iOS Shortcuts app for free.

Does WhatsApp have a built-in scheduler?

WhatsApp Business has a built-in scheduler for quick replies and away messages. Personal WhatsApp doesn't have native scheduling, but you can use device features.

How do I schedule a WhatsApp message on iPhone?

Use the iOS Shortcuts app to create an automation that sends a WhatsApp message at a specific time. You can set up personal automations with time-based triggers.
