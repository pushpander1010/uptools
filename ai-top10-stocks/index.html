Frequently Asked Questions

How are picks chosen?

Candidates are ranked using technical trend (SMA50/200, RSI), pattern context (near support/breakout), basic valuation (PE, dividend), and news sentiment from Yahoo Finance. The combined score determines the top picks.

Which markets are supported?

NSE, BSE, NASDAQ, NYSE, LSE (London), TSX (Toronto), TSE (Tokyo), SSE (Shanghai), HKEX (Hong Kong), and XETRA/Frankfurt — all via Yahoo Finance symbol suffix mapping.

How often does it update?

The daily snapshot refreshes once every 24 hours automatically. Use the Refresh button to force a fresh compute.
