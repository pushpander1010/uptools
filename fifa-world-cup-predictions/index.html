❓ FAQ

When is the FIFA World Cup 2026?
The 2026 FIFA World Cup will be held from June 11 to July 19, 2026, across 16 cities in the United States, Mexico, and Canada.
How many teams are in WC 2026?
For the first time, 48 teams will compete — expanded from 32. They are split into 12 groups of 4 teams each. The top 2 from each group plus the 4 best third-placed teams advance to the Round of 32.
Can I save my predictions?
Yes! Click the "Save Predictions" button — your picks are stored in your browser's local storage and persist even after closing the page.
Can I share my predictions with friends?
Absolutely! Click "Share" to generate a unique link with all your predictions encoded. Send it to friends and see who makes the best picks!
What are the Golden Boot and Golden Ball?
The Golden Boot is awarded to the tournament's top goal scorer. The Golden Ball goes to the best overall player. Both are prestigious individual honors at the World Cup.
Will more teams be added?
This list features all 48 qualified teams as of the latest qualifiers. Teams may change if qualification results shift — we'll update the tool accordingly.
