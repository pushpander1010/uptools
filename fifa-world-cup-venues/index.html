Frequently Asked Questions

How many venues hosted FIFA World Cup 2026 matches? +
The 2026 World Cup was hosted across 16 venues in three countries: 11 in the United States, 3 in Mexico, and 2 in Canada. This was the first time the tournament spanned three nations.
Where is the FIFA World Cup 2026 final being held? +
The 2026 FIFA World Cup Final was played at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey (New York/New Jersey area) on July 19, 2026. Spain won 1–0 against Argentina.
Which stadium is hosting the opening match of World Cup 2026? +
Estadio Azteca in Mexico City hosted the opening match, making history as the first stadium to host three World Cup opening matches — previously hosting the 1970 and 1986 openers.
Which venue is hosting the most matches in World Cup 2026? +
AT&T Stadium in Arlington, Texas (Dallas area) hosted 9 matches — the most of any venue in the tournament.
What is the combined capacity of all World Cup 2026 venues? +
The combined seating capacity across all 16 venues exceeds 1.03 million. The largest is Estadio Azteca (80,824) and the smallest is BMO Field in Toronto (43,036).
Are all World Cup 2026 stadiums outdoor or indoor? +
Most venues are open-air stadiums. Indoor or retractable-roof venues include AT&T Stadium, SoFi Stadium, NRG Stadium, Mercedes-Benz Stadium, and BC Place.

Stadium Visit Tips

✓ Do's

  • Book tickets early — matches will sell out fast
  • Check stadium bag policies before arriving
  • Arrive at least 2 hours before kickoff
  • Use public transit — parking is limited
  • Carry a clear stadium-approved bag

✗ Don'ts

  • Don't bring large backpacks or luggage
  • Don't attempt to bring outside food/drinks
  • Don't use drones near the stadium
  • Don't buy tickets from unauthorized resellers
  • Don't bring professional camera equipment
