❓ Fantasy Points Scoring Rules

Batting Points

• 1 point per run
• 1 bonus point per boundary (4)
• 2 bonus points per six (6)
• 8 points for scoring 50 runs
• 16 points for scoring 100 runs
• Strike rate bonus: +6 pts (SR 170-200), +4 pts (SR 150-170), +2 pts (SR 130-150)

Bowling Points

• 25 points per wicket
• 8 bonus points for 3 wickets
• 16 bonus points for 4 wickets
• 24 bonus points for 5 wickets
• 12 points per maiden over
• Economy rate bonus: +6 pts (ER 5-6), +4 pts (ER 6-7), +2 pts (ER 7-8)

Fielding Points

• 8 points per catch
• 12 points per stumping
• 6 points per run-out (direct hit or assist)
• 4 bonus points for 3 catches
