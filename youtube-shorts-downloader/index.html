📋 How to Download YouTube Shorts

  1. Open YouTube and find the Shorts video you want to download.
  2. Tap the Share button (arrow icon) on the Shorts video.
  3. Copy the link by tapping "Copy link" from the share menu.
  4. Paste the URL into the input field above.
  5. Click "Get Download Links" and wait for processing.
  6. Select quality and click the download button for your preferred option.
