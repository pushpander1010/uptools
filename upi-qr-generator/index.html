About UPI QR Code Generator

This tool generates QR codes compatible with all Indian UPI apps including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay, and WhatsApp Pay. The QR code contains your UPI ID and optional payment details.

📘 How UPI QR codes work
  • UPI QR codes follow the NPCI EMV standard for QR payments.
  • The QR code encodes: UPI ID, amount (optional), and note (optional).
  • When scanned, the UPI app pre-fills the payment details for quick transfer.
  • You can print the QR code at your shop for accepting payments.
🏦 Compatible UPI apps
  • Google Pay (GPay): Scan & Pay, UPI QR
  • PhonePe: Scan & Pay, UPI QR
  • Paytm: Scan & Pay, UPI QR
  • BHIM: Scan QR
  • Amazon Pay: Scan & Pay
  • WhatsApp Pay: Scan QR
  • All major Indian bank apps

📐 How This Tool Works

The tool generates a UPI string in the format:

upi://pay?pa=UPI_ID&pn=NAME&am=AMOUNT&cu=INR

Then it converts this string into a QR code image using the QRCode.js library.

  • pa: UPI ID (payee address)
  • pn: Payee name (optional)
  • am: Amount in INR (optional)
  • cu: Currency (INR)
  • tn: Transaction note (optional)

✅ Do's and ❌ Don'ts for UPI QR Codes

✅ Do's

  • ✅ Do verify the UPI ID before sharing the QR code.
  • ✅ Do use a clear, high-quality QR code for printing.
  • ✅ Do place the QR code in a well-lit, visible location.
  • ✅ Do regularly check your UPI transaction history.

❌ Don'ts

  • ❌ Don't share your UPI PIN with anyone.
  • ❌ Don't use blurry or damaged QR codes for payments.
  • ❌ Don't scan QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources.
  • ❌ Don't place your QR code where it can be easily tampered with.
