Frequently Asked Questions

How is Snapchat score calculated?

Snapchat score is calculated based on: snaps sent (+1 point each), snaps received (+1 point each), stories posted (+1 point each), and bonus points for maintaining streaks and being active. The exact algorithm is proprietary.

What is a good Snapchat score?

Average scores: 50,000-200,000 is typical for regular users. 200,000-500,000 is high. 500,000+ is very high. 1 million+ is exceptional. Your score depends on how long you've used Snapchat and how active you are.

Can you increase Snapchat score fast?

To increase score: send snaps to multiple friends daily, maintain streaks, post stories regularly, open snaps quickly, and stay active. Avoid spam - Snapchat may penalize suspicious activity.
