Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why can't I download a video?

Some videos may be private or restricted. Make sure you have access to the video and that it's not deleted or removed.

Q: What's the maximum file size?

There's no file size limit. You can download videos of any length, but larger files will take longer to download.

Q: Can I download live streams?

Yes, you can download recorded live streams. However, ongoing live streams cannot be downloaded until they're finished.
