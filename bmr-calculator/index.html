📖 Which Formula Should I Use?

Mifflin-St Jeor is the most accurate for most people and is recommended by the American Dietetic Association. Harris-Benedict is an older formula, slightly less accurate. Katch-McArdle is the most accurate if you know your body fat %, as it accounts for lean mass.
