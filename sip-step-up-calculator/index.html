About SIP Step-Up

Step-up SIP means increasing your monthly investment by a fixed percentage each year. This aligns with salary increments and creates significantly more wealth through compounding.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good step-up percentage?

10-10% annual step-up is ideal. Even 10% step-up can more than double your corpus compared to regular SIP over 20 years.

When should I increase my SIP?

Ideally at the start of each financial year or when you receive a salary increment. Automate it with your bank's SIP step-up feature.

Does step-up SIP work with any mutual fund?

Yes, step-up works with all mutual fund types. However, equity funds benefit more due to higher expected returns and longer compounding.

✅ Do's and ❌ Don'ts

✅ Do's

  • ✅ Do start step-up SIP early — compounding works best over time.
  • ✅ Do align step-up with your annual salary increment.
  • ✅ Do review and rebalance your portfolio annually.
  • ✅ Do use step-up to reach retirement goals faster.

❌ Don'ts

  • ❌ Don't skip step-up years — consistency is key.
  • ❌ Don't invest beyond your capacity — maintain emergency fund first.
  • ❌ Don't panic during market downturns — stay invested.
  • ❌ Don't forget to diversify across fund categories.
