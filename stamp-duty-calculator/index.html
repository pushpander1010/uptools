Frequently Asked Questions

What is UK Stamp Duty?

Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) is a tax paid when buying property in England and Northern Ireland. Rates are tiered: 0% up to £250K, 5% on £250K-£925K, 10% on £925K-£1.5M, 12% above £1.5M.

Do first-time buyers pay stamp duty?

First-time buyers get relief: no stamp duty on properties up to £425K, and 5% on the portion from £425K-£625K. Properties over £625K pay standard rates.

When do I pay stamp duty?

Stamp duty must be paid within 14 days of completion. Your solicitor typically handles this payment as part of the conveyancing process.
