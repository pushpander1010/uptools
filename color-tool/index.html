📐 How Color Picker Works

All conversions happen locally in your browser using mathematical formulas. No data is sent anywhere. Palettes use color theory: complementary (opposite on wheel), analogous (adjacent), and triadic (120° apart).

What formats are supported?

HEX, RGB, HSL, and CMYK.

Can I use the eyedropper?

Yes, on Chrome/Edge — click the Eyedropper button.

Is this tool free?
