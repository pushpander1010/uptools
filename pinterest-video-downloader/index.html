How to Download Pinterest Videos & Pins

  1. Find the pin or video you want to download on Pinterest
  2. Click on the pin to open it in full view
  3. Copy the URL from the address bar
  4. Paste the URL into the input field above
  5. Select your preferred quality (Original, 1080p, 720p, or Thumbnail)
  6. Click "Download" to save the pin or video

What You Can Download

  • Video Pins: Animated pins with video content
  • Image Pins: Static image pins
  • Carousel Pins: Multi-image pins (downloads first image)
  • Story Pins: Multi-page story content
  • Idea Pins: Pinterest's native video format

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I download private pins?

No, only public pins can be downloaded. Private pins are restricted by Pinterest's privacy settings.

Q: What's the maximum resolution?

We support downloading pins in their original resolution. Most pins are available in 1080p or higher.

Q: Can I download carousel pins?

Yes, we download the first image from carousel pins. For all images, you'll need to download each pin individually.
