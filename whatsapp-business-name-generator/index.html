💡 Tips for a Great WhatsApp Business Name

  • Keep it short: Under 25 characters for better visibility
  • Make it memorable: Easy to spell and pronounce
  • Add emojis: 1-2 relevant emojis help you stand out
  • Include location: Helps local customers find you
  • Be professional: Avoid slang or confusing abbreviations
  • Test it: Ask friends if they understand what you do

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose a good WhatsApp Business name?

Choose a name that's memorable, easy to spell, reflects your business, and is professional. Keep it under 25 characters for better visibility in chats.

Can I use emojis in my WhatsApp Business name?

Yes! Emojis can make your business name stand out in chat lists. Use 1-2 relevant emojis that represent your business category.

Should my WhatsApp Business name match my company name?

It's recommended but not required. Your WhatsApp Business name should be recognizable to customers. You can add location or service type for clarity.
