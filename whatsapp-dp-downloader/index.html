❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get someone's WhatsApp phone number?

You can find it in your WhatsApp contacts, or ask the person directly. The number should include the country code (e.g., +1 for USA).

What's the maximum resolution?

WhatsApp profile pictures are typically 640×640 pixels. Our tool extracts the highest quality available.

Can I download group profile pictures?

Yes! The same method works for group profile pictures. Just use the group's phone number or link.

Is this tool safe and private?

Yes! We don't store any data. All processing happens on your device. Your privacy is protected.

What if the download doesn't work?

Make sure the phone number is correct and includes the country code. If the contact has hidden their DP, it won't be available.
