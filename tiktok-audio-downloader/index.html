How to Download TikTok Audio

  1. Find the TikTok video with the audio you want to download
  2. Copy the video URL from the address bar
  3. Paste the URL into the input field above
  4. Select your preferred audio quality (320, 256, 192, or 128 kbps)
  5. Click "Download Audio" to save as MP3
  6. The audio file will be saved to your device
