How red-card suspensions work

  • Straight red: automatic 1-match ban.
  • Two yellows: 1-match ban (accumulated).
  • Violent conduct: disciplinary committee can extend to 3+ matches.
  • Semi-final red: a player misses the final if his team reaches it.

Frequently asked questions

What's the ban for a red card?

A straight red = automatic one-match ban. Violent conduct can add more via the disciplinary committee.

Who had the most red cards?

The discipline table ranks teams by total cards. Check the tracker for live standings.

Can a red in the semi-final cost the final?

Yes — if the player's team reaches the final, his ban carries to that match.
