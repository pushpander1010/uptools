How to Download X/Twitter Videos

  1. Find the tweet with the video you want to download
  2. Click on the tweet to open it
  3. Copy the URL from the address bar
  4. Paste the URL into the input field above
  5. Select your preferred quality (1080p, 720p, 480p, or audio)
  6. Click "Download" to save the video
