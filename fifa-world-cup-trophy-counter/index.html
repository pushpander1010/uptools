FAQ

Which country has won the most FIFA World Cups?
Brazil holds the record with 5 World Cup titles (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002). They are the only team to have participated in every tournament.
How many World Cups has Germany won?
Germany has won 4 World Cups (1954 as West Germany, 1974 as West Germany, 1990 as West Germany, and 2014). This ties them with Italy for the second-most titles.
Who won the 2022 FIFA World Cup?
Argentina won the 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar, defeating France 3-3 (4-2 on penalties) in one of the greatest finals in tournament history, led by Lionel Messi.
When did the first FIFA World Cup take place?
The first FIFA World Cup was held in 1930 in Uruguay. Host nation Uruguay won the tournament, defeating Argentina 4-2 in the final.
How many countries have won the World Cup?
Eight countries have won the FIFA World Cup: Brazil (5), Germany (4), Italy (4), Argentina (3), France (2), Uruguay (2), England (1), and Spain (1).
Has any team won back-to-back World Cups?
Yes, two nations have successfully defended the title: Italy (1934, 1938) and Brazil (1958, 1962). No team has won three in a row.
