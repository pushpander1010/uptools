❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a standard tip percentage?

In the US and Canada, standard tip percentages are 15% for adequate service, 18-20% for good service, and 20%+ for excellent service. In Europe, tipping customs vary by country, typically ranging from 5-10%.

How do you split a bill with different tip amounts?

Enter the total bill amount, select your tip percentage, and enter the number of people. The calculator will divide the total (bill + tip) equally among all people.

Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?

It's generally recommended to tip on the pre-tax amount, though many people tip on the total including tax. This calculator works with whatever amount you enter.
